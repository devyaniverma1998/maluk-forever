Privacy Policy

How do we collect your information?

At Malukforever, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. We collect information directly from you when you make a purchase, create an account, sign up for our newsletter, or contact our customer support team. This may include your name, email address, shipping address, phone number, payment information, and details about the products you purchase. We also collect certain information automatically when you visit our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and browsing behavior using cookies to enhance your shopping experience.

How do we use your information?

The information we collect is used to process your orders, deliver the beauty products you’ve selected, and communicate with you about your purchases. We may also use your data to send you promotional offers, product recommendations, beauty tips, and other marketing materials that you’ve opted into. Your personal information is used solely for the purposes for which it was collected, such as processing transactions, improving customer service, and enhancing your shopping experience with us.

How do we protect your information?

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We use secure servers and encryption protocols to protect sensitive information such as payment details and personal data. Your information is stored in secure networks, and only authorized personnel have access to this data. We also utilize Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt your credit card information during transmission, ensuring that your data remains confidential.

Do we share your information with third parties?

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without your consent. However, we may share your data with trusted third-party partners and service providers who assist us with processing payments, managing our website, and fulfilling orders. These third parties are required to handle your information securely and in compliance with privacy laws. We may also disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights.

What cookies do we use?

We use cookies to improve your shopping experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that allow us to remember your preferences, keep track of your shopping cart, and personalize the content you see. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that some features of our website may not function properly without them.

How can you update or delete your information?

You have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we have on file. You can easily do this by logging into your account and managing your account settings. If you need assistance or would like to request the complete deletion of your account and data, please contact our customer service team, and we will ensure that your request is processed promptly.

How do we handle children's privacy?

Our website and services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have unintentionally collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information. If you believe we may have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify our privacy policy at any time. If we make significant changes, we will post the updated policy on this page and update the date of the revision. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your privacy and manage your personal data.

Contacting Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices or how we handle your personal data, please don't hesitate to contact us at [Contact Information]. We're happy to help and ensure your privacy is respected!
