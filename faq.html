Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  1. Home
  2. /
  3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What makes Maluk Forever products unique?

Maluk Forever products are crafted with the finest natural, plant-based ingredients, designed to nourish and enhance your skin's health. We focus on creating effective, gentle formulas that are safe for all skin types.

2. Can I use your products if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, our products are designed to be gentle and suitable for sensitive skin. We recommend doing a patch test before use.

3. Are your products suitable for all skin types?

Yes, our products are designed for all skin types, including dry, oily, combination, and sensitive skin. We offer a range of products tailored to different skincare needs and concerns.

4. How do I choose the right products for my skin?

Browse our product guides or contact our customer support for personalized recommendations based on your skin type.

5. Can I return or exchange products?

Yes, you can return or exchange products within 5 days of delivery. Please ensure the product is unused and in its original condition. Returns or exchanges are not accepted for used items.

6. How can I contact "Maluk Forever"?

For any queries, complaints, or suggestions, feel free to contact our customer support (10am to 6pm, Mon-Sat).

Call or WhatsApp us at 85888 01711.

7. How do I check if the product suits me?

We recommend performing a patch test before using any product, even though all our products are made with natural ingredients. Skin reactions can vary based on individual sensitivities.

8. Can I return products if they don’t suit me?

We do not accept returns if a product doesn't suit your skin type. When purchasing, it’s your choice, and our experts recommend products based on your specific concerns.

9. What if the amount is deducted twice from my account?

If you're charged twice for the same order, please contact our customer support team with your payment proof, and we’ll assist you in resolving the issue.

10. I made a payment but didn’t receive any confirmation message. What should I do?

This can happen due to technical issues or slow server response. Please reach out to our customer support team with payment details so we can assist you further.

11. How can I return my product?

For returns, please refer to our Return/Refund Policy for the full procedure and eligibility.

12. Can I cancel my order or change any product in my order?

Once an order is placed, it cannot be modified, cancelled, or refunded.

13. What if I'm facing an issue while placing an order?

If you're encountering any issues, our customer support team is ready to assist you. Please get in touch, and we'll help resolve the matter.

14. Can I change the address after the order is shipped?

Once an order is marked as “Ready to ship,” we are unable to change the shipping address.

15. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including debit/credit cards, net banking, UPI, Paytm, and more.

16. Are your products safe to use during pregnancy?

While our products are made from safe, natural ingredients, we recommend consulting with your physician or gynecologist before use during pregnancy.

17. What is the shelf life of your products?

Our products are tested to ensure they are safe for use up to 2 years from the manufacturing date. We recommend using them within this period for optimal effectiveness.

18. Do you test your products on animals?

No, we do not test our products on animals, nor do we use any animal by-products. We are committed to cruelty-free practices.

19. Can children use your products?

Yes, our baby care range is suitable for children aged 0-14 years. Most of our other products are suitable for individuals 16 years and older. Please consult our experts for specific recommendations.

20. Do you have products designed for men?

Our products are unisex and suitable for both men and women.

21. Do your products have strong fragrances?

Most of our products have natural fragrances, and some are enhanced with essential oils to provide a pleasant scent.

22. Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

We offer products formulated for different skin types, including sensitive skin. We still recommend a patch test before use and encourage you to consult our experts for personalized advice.

23. Do you use preservatives in your products?

Our products are made from natural ingredients, and we use safe, natural preservatives in minimal quantities to maintain their shelf life.

24. What should I do if my order shows as delivered, but I haven’t received it?

If your order shows as delivered but you haven’t received it, please contact us within 48 hours of the delivery notification. After 48 hours, we cannot assume liability. We will investigate the issue with our logistics partner, and this may take 7-10 business days.

25. How do you ensure my products are not damaged during delivery?

We take great care in packing our products. They are wrapped in bubble wrap and placed in sturdy boxes to prevent damage during shipping. Our goal is to deliver your products in perfect condition.
