Shipping Policy

How does the delivery process work?

Once your order is successfully processed, we thoroughly inspect the products to ensure they are in perfect condition. After passing our quality check, the items are carefully packed and handed over to our trusted delivery partner. Our delivery partners will ensure your package reaches you as quickly as possible. If they are unable to deliver due to an incorrect address or timing issues, they will contact you to resolve the matter.

How are items packaged?

We are committed to sustainable practices, and this includes our packaging. Malukforever uses eco-friendly cardboard packaging made from 100% recycled paper. Where possible, we opt for paper-fillers instead of plastic bubble wrap. This is part of our ongoing efforts to minimize our environmental impact. We continue to explore new, innovative solutions to further reduce plastic use while ensuring your order’s safety.

What locations do you ship to?

Currently, Malukforever ships only within [Country] and does not offer international shipping. We are focused on delivering to a wide range of locations within the country and will continue to expand our shipping services in the future.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation message via email and SMS. You can track the status of your order by visiting the "Track My Order" section on our website or by using the tracking link provided. Additionally, you can opt to receive order updates via WhatsApp for your convenience.

What is the estimated delivery time?

Most orders are dispatched within 1-4 business days, excluding weekends and public holidays. However, some products may need to be sourced directly from the brand or vendor, which could cause a slight delay. If you’re placing an order during a sale or promotional event, please note that delivery may take longer due to high demand. Our goal is to dispatch all orders within 5 business days in such cases.

Will my order be shipped in multiple shipments?

This can happen when items in your order are sourced from different warehouses or suppliers. Don’t worry! You will only be charged shipping fees on the first package that arrives, and any subsequent packages will be delivered at no extra shipping cost.

Are there any shipping charges?

Shipping charges are based on the total value of your order and your location.

  • For new customers: Free shipping on orders above ₹299.
  • For returning customers: Free shipping on orders above ₹149.

Please note that we currently do not accept Cash on Delivery (COD) for orders below ₹249 or above ₹50,000.

Does Malukforever ship outside of [Country]?

At this time, Malukforever does not offer international shipping. We are focused on providing excellent service within [Country] and hope to expand our shipping options in the future.
