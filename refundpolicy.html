Refund Policy

Introduction

At Malukforever, we want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. If you are not completely satisfied or if there was an issue with your order, we are here to help. This Refund Policy outlines the steps to request a refund and the conditions under which refunds are issued.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are eligible in the following cases:

  • The product you received is damaged, defective, or faulty.
  • The product you received is not as described or was the wrong item.
  • If you are unable to use the product due to a shipping error or other issues related to our fulfillment process.

Refunds are not eligible for:

  • Products that were damaged due to improper use or handling after delivery.
  • Products that were not returned in their original condition, including used or opened skincare, makeup, and hygiene-related items.
  • Products purchased during a sale or promotional event, unless explicitly stated in the offer terms.

How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team within [number] days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number, a detailed description of the issue, and any supporting evidence (e.g., photos of damaged or incorrect items).
  3. Our team will assess your request and guide you through the next steps.

Refund Process

Once your refund request is approved, we will process your refund to the original payment method. The refund may take [X] business days to reflect in your account, depending on your payment provider.

If you paid using a credit card or other online payment method, the refund will be credited back to the same payment method used for the purchase. For other payment methods, such as bank transfers or cash on delivery, we will discuss alternate arrangements with you.

Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may issue a partial refund, such as when only part of an order is faulty or damaged, or if you choose to return part of the order. The amount refunded will depend on the condition of the returned items and the value of your order.

Refunds for Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a damaged or defective product, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a full refund or send you a replacement at no extra cost. Please provide photos of the damaged product and packaging for our records.

Refunds for Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, certain products such as skincare, cosmetics, and personal care items are non-returnable. However, if you receive a defective or incorrect item from our store, we will still process a full refund or offer an exchange, as per our policy for damaged or incorrect goods.

Order Cancellations and Refunds

If you cancel an order before it has been shipped, we will issue a full refund. Once your order has been shipped, you will need to follow our standard return process to receive a refund. Please refer to our Return Policy for more details.

Processing Time

Refunds are processed within [X] business days after we have received the returned product, or after approval of your refund request if the product was never shipped. Depending on the payment method used, the time it takes for the refund to appear on your statement may vary. Please allow up to [X] additional business days for your payment provider to process the refund.

Amendments to This Refund Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. It is your responsibility to review this policy periodically to stay informed about any updates. By continuing to use our services after such changes, you consent to the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact our customer service team at [Customer Service Email/Phone Number]. We are here to help and ensure your satisfaction.
