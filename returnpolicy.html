Return Policy

Introduction

Thank you for shopping at Malukforever. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with the items you have purchased, please review our Return Policy below.

Eligibility for Returns

You can return most products within [number] days from the date of delivery for a full refund or exchange, provided they meet the following conditions:

  • The product must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • The product must be in its original packaging with all tags, labels, and accessories intact.
  • Skincare, makeup, and other hygiene-related items are non-returnable unless defective or damaged.

Non-Returnable Items

Some products cannot be returned due to hygiene or safety reasons, including but not limited to:

  • Opened skincare products
  • Makeup products
  • Personal care items
  • Gift cards

If you are unsure whether a product is returnable, please contact our customer support team for clarification.

How to Initiate a Return

If you would like to return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account and navigate to the “Orders” section.
  2. Select the order and the items you wish to return.
  3. Click on “Return Item” and follow the instructions to complete the process.
  4. If you are unable to process the return via the website, please contact our customer service team at [Customer Service Email/Phone Number].

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the product is defective or damaged upon arrival. In such cases, we will cover the return shipping costs. Please ensure that the product is securely packaged to prevent any damage during the return transit.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned product, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will be applied to your original payment method within [X] business days. Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange a product, we recommend initiating a return for a refund and placing a new order for the desired item. Currently, we do not offer direct exchanges. Please ensure the returned product meets the conditions listed above to be eligible for a refund.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact our customer service team immediately at [Customer Service Email/Phone Number]. We will arrange for a return and issue a full refund or send a replacement at no extra cost to you. Please provide us with photos of the damaged product and packaging for verification purposes.

Order Cancellations

If you wish to cancel your order, please contact us immediately at [Customer Service Email/Phone Number]. Once the order has been processed or shipped, it may no longer be eligible for cancellation. If your order has already been shipped, you can initiate a return following the process outlined above.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Return Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about our return procedures.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Return Policy or need assistance with a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team at [Customer Service Email/Phone Number]. We are here to help!
