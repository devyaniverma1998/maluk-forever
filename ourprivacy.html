Our Policy

  1. Home

At Maluk Forever, we are committed to ensuring a seamless and secure shopping experience for all of our valued customers. By shopping with us, you acknowledge and agree to the following policies. Please read through our policies carefully to understand how we operate and what you can expect when purchasing our products.

Shipping Policy

We strive to deliver your orders as quickly as possible while ensuring a secure and efficient process.

  • Order Processing Time: All orders are processed and shipped within 48 hours of placing the order. We partner with trusted logistics providers to deliver your products in a timely manner.
  • Delivery Time: We aim to deliver your order within 15 working days from the receipt of payment and order confirmation, depending on the shipping destination. Delivery times may vary based on location and courier service.
  • Out-of-Stock Items: If an item is out of stock at the time of purchase, we may require an additional 7-10 days to restock the product and fulfill your order.

Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to love your purchase, but we understand that sometimes things don’t work out. Here’s how we handle returns and exchanges:

  • Non-returnable Items: Due to the personal care and wellness nature of our products, all items are non-returnable, including beauty, skincare, and consumable goods.
  • Damaged or Incorrect Items: If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact our customer service within 48 hours of delivery. You must provide a 360-degree unboxing video along with clear pictures of the products, packaging, and invoice for us to process your claim.
  • Exchanges due to Allergic Reactions or Personal Preference: We do not accept exchanges for reasons such as allergic reactions, personal dislikes, or other subjective reasons. We always recommend patch testing before use, as stated on our product packaging.
  • Return Process: Please ensure the item is unused, unopened, and in original packaging if you are making a claim. No refund will be issued under any circumstances.

Refund Policy

At Maluk Forever, we have a no-refund policy for most purchases, as our products are non-returnable and non-exchangeable due to hygiene and health regulations. However, we will assist with any issues as per the guidelines below:

  • Order Cancellation: Once an order is placed, it cannot be modified, canceled, or refunded.
  • Failed Delivery or Lost Orders: If your order is marked as Lost by our logistics partner, we will resend the same item to you. We will not issue a refund in such cases.
  • Refund Process for Canceled Orders: If we need to cancel an order for any reason (including a prepaid order), we will process a refund within 24 hours and the amount will be credited back to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.
  • Wrong Product Received: If you receive the wrong product, please contact our customer service team immediately. We will initiate the return process and ensure the correct product is shipped to you.

Privacy Policy

Your policy is important to us. By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in our policy policy.

  • Information Collection: We collect basic user details such as first name, last name, and email. This information is stored securely on our server to facilitate your orders and communications with us.
  • Data Deletion: If you wish to delete your account and personal information, you can contact our customer support team at any time. Requests for data deletion will be processed within 90 days, and you will receive confirmation once completed.
